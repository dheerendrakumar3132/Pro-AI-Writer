Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the AI Writing Tool provided by Novaplexes ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you provide when using our AI-based email, resume, and report generation services.

2. Information We Collect

We operate on a "No-Storage" policy. We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide personal identification to use the tool.

  • Input Data: The text details (prompts) you enter for generating content (e.g., leave details, resume points) are collected temporarily solely for processing.
  • Usage Data: Like most websites, our hosting server may collect standard log files (IP address, browser type) for security and maintenance purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you enter is used strictly to generate the requested document. We do not store, save, or resell your input data or the generated output in any database. Once you close or refresh the page, your data is wiped from the browser view.

4. Third-Party Services (AI Processing)

To generate high-quality content, we utilize the Google Gemini API. When you click "Generate," your text input is securely transmitted to Google's servers for processing and returned immediately. By using this tool, you acknowledge that your input data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement standard SSL encryption (HTTPS) to ensure that the data transmitted between your browser and our server is secure. However, please do not enter highly sensitive personal information (such as bank passwords or credit card numbers) into the AI generation fields.
