Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences (such as login status or language selection) over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them.

2. How We Use Cookies

At Novaplexes, we use cookies to improve your experience and ensure our AI tool functions correctly. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to work (e.g., security features, balancing traffic). The website cannot function properly without these.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: We may use third-party tools (like Google Analytics) to understand how many people are using our tool and which pages are most popular. This data is anonymous.
  • Advertising Cookies (Future Use): In the future, we may partner with Google AdSense to show advertisements. These third-party cookies track your browsing habits to show you relevant ads.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Since we use the Google Gemini API for generating text, Google may place cookies on your browser in accordance with their own policies. We do not control these third-party cookies. We recommend reviewing Google’s Cookie Policy.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. You can control this through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check this page regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.
